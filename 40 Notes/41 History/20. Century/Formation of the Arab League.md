The [[Arab League]] was formally formed in [[Cairo]] 0n March 22, 1945, initially with six members: [[Egypt]], [[Iraq]], [[Transjordan]], [[Lebanon]], [[Saudi Arabia]] and [[Syria]]. Currently, the league has 22 members.
